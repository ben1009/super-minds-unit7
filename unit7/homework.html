Present Continuous

2026年3月14日 · 语法专题

§ 语法结构

结构：人/物 + am/is/are + 动词-ing + 其他

👧 She is playing basketball.
👦 He is playing basketball.
🐱 It is playing basketball.
👥 They are playing basketball.
🎭 You are playing basketball.
👥 We are playing basketball.
🙋 I am playing basketball.

结构：人/物 + am/is/are + not + 动词-ing + 其他

🙅 I am not eating breakfast.
🚫 She is not playing basketball.
🚗 I am not driving.
📚 I am not having a class now.

结构：Am/Is/Are + 人/物 + 动词-ing + 其他?

Q: Are you eating breakfast?
Yes, I am. No, I am not.
Q: Is he kicking the ball?
Yes, he is. No, he isn't.
Q: Are you flying a kite? 你在放风筝吗？
Yes, I am. 是的，我正在放风筝 🪁 No, I am not. 不是，我没在放风筝

故 Story: The Bus Trip 乘大巴去旅行 · 完整版

1

The Bus Trip

乘大巴去旅行

今天我们来讲述一次乘大巴去海滩的旅行故事。

2

A day at the beach

海滩一日游

excited: 激动的

"We are going to the beach! We are very excited!"

我们要去海滩了！我们很激动！

3

Off we go!

我们出发了！

大巴开动了！

大家兴奋地说："Off we go!"（我们走啦！）

4

Moving

正在开动 / 行驶中

现在进行时状态描述

The bus is moving.

大巴正在行驶。

🚌 车动起来了！We are moving!

⚠️ 注意：moving 表示"正在移动/开动"

5

Let's ask the driver

我们去问司机吧

旅途中遇到问题，询问司机

"Excuse me, how long is the trip?"

6

There be 句型观察

描述看到的景象（点击展开查看全部例句）

路上看到了什么：

🐑 There are lots of sheep on the road.
路上有很多羊
👥 There are lots of people in the park.
公园里有很多人
🐱 There is a cat on the floor.
地板上有一只猫
🚁 There are lots of helicopters in the sky.
天空中有很多直升机
🚁 There is a helicopter in the sky.
天空中有一架直升机
💡 语法提示：
There is + 单数名词
There are + 复数名词
7

We have got a problem!

我们遇到了麻烦！

⚠️ tyre: 轮胎

We have got a problem with the tyre.

我们的轮胎出了问题了。

🧍
I have got a problem with my neck.

我的脖子不舒服。

🚗
They have got a problem with their car.

他们的车出了问题。

🚽
We have got a problem with our toilet.

我们的马桶坏了。

8

Here we are!

我们到了！

🎉 终于到达目的地！

经过一路波折，大巴终于到达了海滩。

9

This is the bus to...

这是去...的大巴

This is the bus to the airport.

这是去机场的大巴。

🚌 This is the bus to the zoo.

🚌 This is the bus to the station.

🚌 This is the bus to school.

10

Now it is my turn...

轮到我...了

Now it is my turn to help you.

轮到我帮你了。

Now it is my turn to take care of our cat.

轮到我照顾我们的猫了。

句型：It is one's turn to do something
轮到某人做某事了
