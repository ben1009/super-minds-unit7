Present Continuous

掌握"正在投球"的动作表达，通过棒球汉堡结构轻松学习英语语法

核心结构："棒球汉堡"公式

🍞

上面包

主语 (谁?)

I, You, He
She, It, We
They...

⚾

be动词

am/is/are

🍞

下面包

动词 -ing

pitching
batting
running...

🧤

句子构建演示

She + is + pitching + the ball

She is pitching the ball. ⚾

她正在投球。

Be动词选择口诀

👤 我是 am → I am pitching.
👥 你是 are → You are batting.
👤‍👤‍👤 is 连着他她它 → He/She/It is running.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 复数全部都用 are → We/They are playing.

速查表

I am → I am catching.
He/She/It is → He is fielding.
You/We/They are → We are winning.

三种基本句型

肯定句

主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing + 其他

⚾ She is playing baseball.

🏏 We are having a practice.

否定句

主语 + am/is/are + not + 动词-ing + 其他

❌ I am not playing today.

🚫 She isn't batting now.

疑问句

Am/Is/Are + 主语 + 动词-ing...?

❓ Are you throwing?

❓ What are they doing?

互动练习 (点击题目查看答案和翻译)

1

What ______ the children ______ (do)? They ______ ______ (play) with the baseball.

答案：are, doing; are playing

他们在做什么？他们正在玩棒球。

2

Look at the pitcher. He ______ (throw) the ball in the stadium.

答案：is throwing

看那个投手，他正在体育场投球。

3

Be quiet, please. The catcher ______ (wait) for an important pitch.

答案：is waiting

请安静，捕手正在等待一个重要的投球。

4

Where is my bat? I ______ (look) for it.

答案：am looking

我的球棒在哪？我正在找它。

5

Listen! The fans ______ (cheer) in the stands.

答案：are cheering

听！球迷们正在看台上欢呼。

6

We can not play now. It ______ (rain).

答案：is raining

我们现在不能比赛，正在下雨。

7

Hurry up! Your teammate ______ (wait) for you at the dugout.

答案：is waiting

快点！你的队友正在球员休息区等你。

补充阅读：Our Trip to the Championship 点击段落查看中文翻译

⚾

Today, we are going to the championship game! We are all very excited for the big match. The team bus is here. "Off we go!" we shout.

今天，我们要去参加冠军赛！我们都对这场大赛非常期待。球队大巴来了。"我们出发吧！"我们喊道。

🚌

The bus starts moving. We look out of the window. "Look! There are lots of fans on the road!" says Tiantian. After a long time, the bus stops. "Are we at the stadium?" asks Claire.

公交车开动了。我们看向窗外。"看！路上有很多球迷！"甜甜说。过了很久，公交车停了下来。"我们到体育场了吗？"克莱尔问。

🔧

"No, look! There are lots of people in the parking lot," I say. "And look! There is a cat on the ground under that tree!" But the bus does not move again. The driver says, "We have got a problem with the tyre." What can we do? The driver smiles and says, "No, this is the bus to the airport. You are on the wrong bus!"

"没有，看！停车场里有很多人，"我说。"还有看！那棵树下的地上有一只猫！"但是公交车再也不动了。司机说："我们的轮胎出了问题。"我们能怎么办？司机笑着说："不，这是去机场的公交车。你们上错车了！"

🏟️

Oh no! But the driver is very kind. He helps us call a new bus. When the new bus comes, the driver says, "Now it is my turn to help you." He shows us the right way. Finally, we see the stadium! "Here we are!" we all shout. Our bus trip is an adventure, and now our championship game can begin!

哦不！但是司机非常好心。他帮我们叫了一辆新车。当新车来时，司机说："现在轮到我帮助你们了。"他给我们指了正确的路。最后，我们看到了体育场！"我们到了！"我们都喊道。我们的公交车之旅是一次冒险，现在我们的冠军赛可以开始了！

理解测验 (点击查看答案)

1. Why are the children excited at the beginning of the story?

Because they are going to the championship game. (因为他们要去参加冠军赛。)

2. What do they see on the road that makes the bus trip interesting?

They see lots of fans on the road. (他们在路上看到了很多球迷。)

3. Why does the bus stop and not move again?

Because there is a problem with the tyre. (因为轮胎出了问题。)

4. How do the children find out they are on the wrong bus?

The driver tells them this is the bus to the airport, not to the stadium. (司机告诉他们这是去机场的公交车，不是去体育场的。)

5. How does the story end? Do they get to the stadium?

Yes, the driver helps them call a new bus and they finally see the stadium. (是的，司机帮他们叫了新车，最后他们到达了体育场。)
