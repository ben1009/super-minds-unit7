§ 语法结构 · 投球姿势

肯定句 · 击中球心

结构：人/物 + am/is/are + 动词-ing + 其他

⚾ He is pitching the ball.
🏏 She is swinging the bat.
🧤 I am catching the ball.
🏃 They are running to base.

否定句 · 漏接失误

结构：人/物 + am/is/are + not + 动词-ing + 其他

❌ He is not hitting the ball.
🚫 They are not pitching now.
⛔ I am not playing catcher today.

疑问句 · 战术询问

结构：Am/Is/Are + 人/物 + 动词-ing + 其他?

Q: Is the pitcher throwing fast balls?

Yes, he is. ⚾ No, he isn't. ❌

Q: Are you batting right-handed?

你在用右手击球吗？

Yes, I am. 🏏 No, I'm not. ✋
