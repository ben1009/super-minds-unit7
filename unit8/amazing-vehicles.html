任务1：单词与例句
amazing
令人惊叹的 very surprising or wonderful
"This car has got an amazing swimming pool inside."
because
因为 for the reason that
"I like this bus because it has got a bed and a TV."
inside
在里面 in or into the inner part
"There are a lot of sofas inside the long car."
difficult
困难的 not easy
"This car is difficult to drive."
front
前面 the forward part
"There is a big sofa at the front of the bus."
back
后面 the rear part
"At the back, there are two beds and a TV."
party
聚会 a celebration
"Some people use this bus for special parties."
drive
驾驶 to operate a vehicle
"We often go for a drive in my dad's amazing car."
pool
游泳池 swimming pool
"There is a small pool inside the car."
任务2：Measure 与 There be 句式
Measure vs Measuring
I measure the pencil with a ruler.
I am measuring the length of the pencil.
measure = 动词原形（表示动作）
measuring = 现在分词/动名词
There be 句式 - 长度单位
There are 10 mm in every cm
There are 100 cm in every meter
1 cm = 10 mm
1 m = 100 cm
任务3：阅读理解 & 任务4：完形填空
Reading A: An Amazing Double-Decker Bus

This bus is special because it has two floors. Inside, there is a small cinema on the top floor. You can watch films with your friends while travelling. On the bottom floor, there is a kitchen and a dining area. You can even find a mini garden at the back!

The bus does not take people to school or work. It is used for tours and parties. It needs a special driver because it is very tall and long. Sometimes there are parties inside, and everyone dances to music.

1. How many floors does the bus have?
a) One floor b) Two floors c) Three floors
✓ 正确答案
b) Two floors
文中明确提到"This bus is special because it has two floors"。
2. What can you do on the top floor?
a) Cook dinner b) Watch films c) Dance to music
✓ 正确答案
b) Watch films
文中说"there is a small cinema on the top floor. You can watch films with your friends"。
3. Where is the mini garden?
a) On the top floor b) In the kitchen c) At the back of the bus
✓ 正确答案
c) At the back of the bus
文中明确提到"You can even find a mini garden at the back!"。
4. Why does the bus need a special driver?
a) Because it is very fast b) Because it is very tall and long c) Because it has a swimming pool
✓ 正确答案
b) Because it is very tall and long
文中说"It needs a special driver because it is very tall and long"。
5. When is the bus usually used?
a) For school trips b) For tours and parties c) For going to work
✓ 正确答案
b) For tours and parties
文中明确说明"It is used for tours and parties"，而不是上学或上班。
Reading B: An Amazing Car for Films and Parties

This car is not like a normal car. It is very long, so it cannot go on small roads. Inside, it has everything for a party. You can see comfortable sofas, soft beds, and even a big TV. But the most exciting thing is the swimming pool! Yes, there is a small pool inside the car.

Driving this car is difficult. One driver is not enough because the driver cannot see the back. That is why there are two drivers — one at the front and one at the back. They must work together to turn corners and stop safely.

People do not use this car every day. They use it in films or for special parties. Would you like to ride in it?

1. Why can't this car go on small roads?
a) Because it is too fast b) Because it is very long c) Because it has two drivers
✓ 正确答案
b) Because it is very long
文中说"It is very long, so it cannot go on small roads"。
2. What is the most exciting thing inside the car?
a) The big TV b) The comfortable sofas c) The swimming pool
✓ 正确答案
c) The swimming pool
文中明确提到"But the most exciting thing is the swimming pool!"。
3. How many drivers does this car need?
a) One b) Two c) Three
✓ 正确答案
b) Two
文中说"That is why there are two drivers — one at the front and one at the back"。
4. Why do they need two drivers?
a) Because one driver cannot see the back b) Because the car has two floors c) Because the car goes very fast
✓ 正确答案
a) Because one driver cannot see the back
文中解释"One driver is not enough because the driver cannot see the back"。
5. When do people use this car?
a) Every day for work b) For school trips c) In films or for special parties
✓ 正确答案
c) In films or for special parties
文中最后一段说"They use it in films or for special parties"。
